About

Understanding
the mechanics.

Behavioral patterns repeat. Across different relationships, different careers, different decades — the same loops show up in different circumstances. PatternsShift develops self-guided educational programs built around that reality.

I'm Hanna Pashnik, the founder. 17 years of practice in psychological counselling and psychotherapy across clinical settings in Eastern Europe and the EU. The work draws from CBT, ACT, and Jungian psychology.
